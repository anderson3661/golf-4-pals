Bishop wins at Rusper

Dave Bishop secured a controversial victory at the players 'favourite' course with a par 4 on the 18th hole, despite missing a 4 foot birdie putt by woefully leaving it short.

It was all to play for down the 18th with all 4 competitors within 3 points of each other.

The early pacemaker was Martin Anderson who was 3 over par after 7 holes before succumbing to the Rusper 'forest' holes. Anderson ended up finishing 4th, the first time he has not finished in the top 2 this season.
